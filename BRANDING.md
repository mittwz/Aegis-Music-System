# Branding

## Product name

**Aegis Music System**

## Short name

**Aegis**

## Why this name

In Dota, the Aegis is a symbol of momentum, protection, clutch moments, and second chances. The project uses that association to represent adaptive music that follows the match and reacts to pressure swings.

## Tagline

**Adaptive music direction for Dota 2 and Spotify**

## GitHub repository name

`aegis-music-system`

## Positioning

Aegis Music System is not just a playlist switcher. It is a lightweight real-time music director that interprets Dota 2 match flow and shapes the soundtrack around it.

- clean
